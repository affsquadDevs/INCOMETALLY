# Tax Data & Official Sources

This document records the figures used by IncomeTally's calculators and the official
sources they were verified against (research pass June 2026). Figures are estimates for
a standard single employee unless noted; rates and thresholds change yearly. Where 2026
values are published they are used; otherwise the latest available (2025) values are used
and labelled. See each country page's "Data sources" box for the public-facing list.

> These are simplified estimates, not official assessments or tax advice.

---

## United States (USD)
- Federal brackets, standard deduction, FICA (Social Security 6.2% to wage base; Medicare 1.45% + 0.9% surtax). State/local excluded by default (user-entered estimate).
- Sources: IRS — Federal income tax rates & brackets; IRS Tax Topic 751 (SS & Medicare); IRS Standard Deduction.

## Germany (EUR)
- Progressive ESt 14–45% + Solidaritätszuschlag + church tax; social insurance (health, pension, unemployment, long-term care) split with employer.
- Sources: Bundesfinanzministerium (BMF); Bundeszentralamt für Steuern (BZSt).

## United Kingdom (GBP)
- Personal allowance £12,570 (taper £100k–£125,140); 20/40/45%; NI primary threshold £12,570, UEL £50,270, 8% then 2%; student loan plans 2/4/5.
- Sources: GOV.UK Income Tax rates; GOV.UK National Insurance rates; GOV.UK student loan repayment.

## Poland (PLN)
- PIT: 12% up to 120,000 tax base, 32% above. Tax-free amount 30,000 via tax-reducing amount 3,600/yr. (2025 & 2026 unchanged.)
- Employee ZUS: pension 9.76% + disability 1.5% (capped at annual base: **260,190 (2025) / 282,600 (2026)**) + sickness 2.45% (uncapped). All ZUS deductible from PIT base.
- Health 9% (base = gross − ZUS); NOT tax-deductible since 2022.
- Relief for under-26 (PIT-0): employment income exempt up to 85,528/yr (ZUS/health still due).
- Joint filing: tax = 2 × scale(combined base / 2).
- Child relief (credit): 1,112.04 (1st), 1,112.04 (2nd), 2,000.04 (3rd), 2,700 (4th+).
- Sources: podatki.gov.pl (skala podatkowa, ulga dla młodych, wspólne rozliczenie, ulga na dziecko); biznes.gov.pl + zus.pl (ZUS rates/cap).

## France (EUR) — barème 2026 (2025 income)
- Brackets per part: 0% ≤11,600; 11% to 29,579; 30% to 84,577; 41% to 181,917; 45% above.
- Quotient familial: single 1 part, couple 2; +0.5 per of first two children, +1 from 3rd. Plafonnement 1,807/half-part.
- 10% professional-expense deduction: min 509, max 14,555 per person.
- Décote: single if tax ≤1,982 → 897 − 45.25%×tax; couple if ≤3,277 → 1,483 − 45.25%×tax.
- Employee cotisations: pension 6.90% (≤1 PASS) + 0.40% uncapped; CSG 9.2% (6.8% deductible, 2.4% not) + CRDS 0.5% on 98.25% of gross up to 4 PASS. PASS 48,060 (2026) / 47,040 (2025). Total employee ≈21–23%.
- Sources: service-public.gouv.fr (A18045 barème, F2705/F2702 quotient, F1989 10%, F34328 décote, F2971 CSG/CRDS); urssaf.fr (PASS); cleiss.fr (rate table).

## Spain (EUR) — 2025
- IRPF = state half + regional half. Aggregate scale commonly: 19/24/30/37/45/47% at 12,450/20,200/35,200/60,000/300,000. (State-only manual table is 9.5–24.5%.)
- Regional variation (aggregate top): Madrid ≈45%, Andalucía ≈47%, Cataluña ≈50%, C. Valenciana ≈54%. País Vasco & Navarra = independent foral systems.
- Mínimo personal 5,550; descendants 2,400 / 2,700 / 4,000 / 4,500 (1st–4th), +2,800 under 3. Applied at lowest-bracket rate (not a base deduction).
- Employee SS ≈6.48% (common 4.70% + unemployment 1.55% + training 0.10% + MEI 0.13%); base máxima 4,909.50/mo = 58,914/yr (2025); 5,101.20/mo (2026); MEI worker 0.15% (2026). Deductible.
- Joint filing: base reduction 3,400 (two-parent) / 2,150 (single-parent).
- Sources: AEAT (escala estatal, mínimo personal y familiar, tributación conjunta); BOE (Orden cotización 2025/2026); seg-social.es.

## Italy (EUR)
- IRPEF: 23% ≤28,000; **35% (2025) / 33% (2026)** 28,000–50,000; 43% above.
- Regional addizionale ~0.7–3.33% (varies); municipal addizionale up to 0.8% (Roma 0.9%). Per-region/comune via MEF lookup.
- Employee detrazioni (Art.13 TUIR) create no-tax area ~8,500; 1,955 credit ≤15,000 then taper to 0 at 50,000.
- Employee INPS 9.19% + extra 1% above prima fascia (55,448/yr 2025; 56,224/yr 2026); massimale 120,607 (2025) / 122,295 (2026). Deductible.
- Children under 21 → no detrazione (Assegno Unico); 21–29 → 950/child; spouse credit up to 800.
- Sources: agenziaentrate.gov.it (IRPEF aliquote; circolari 4/E & 6/E 2025); MEF finanze.gov.it (addizionali lookup); INPS (circolari 27/2025, 6/2026).

## Sweden (SEK)
- Municipal tax avg 32.41% (2025) / 32.38% (2026); examples (total kommun+region, 2026): Stockholm 30.55%, Göteborg 32.60%, Malmö 32.42%. Range ~28.93%–35.65%.
- State tax 20% above skiktgräns (taxable): 625,800 (2025) / 643,000 (2026).
- Grundavdrag (income-dependent): under-66 max 45,300 (2025) / 45,600 (2026); high-income floor ~17,300/17,400.
- Allmän pensionsavgift 7% fully offset by matching skattereduktion → ~no net effect.
- Jobbskatteavdrag (earned-income credit, reduces municipal tax only): max under-66 47,923 (2025) / 53,147 (2026). Materially raises take-home.
- Church fee (~1.05%, members only) + burial fee (~0.29%, everyone).
- Sources: Skatteverket (belopp och procent 2025/2026; skiktgränser PDF; grundavdrag; skattereduktioner; begravningsavgift); SCB (kommunalskatterna); Svenska kyrkan (kyrkoavgift).

## Portugal (EUR) — mainland (Continente)
- IRS Art.68 brackets. 2026 (live official): 12.5/15.7/21.2/24.1/31.1/34.9/43.1/44.6/48% at 8,342/12,587/17,838/23,089/29,397/43,090/46,566/86,634. 2025: 12.5/16/21.5/24.4/31.4/34.9/43.1/44.6/48% at 8,059/12,160/17,233/22,306/28,400/41,629/44,987/83,696.
- Specific deduction (employment) = 8.54×IAS = **4,462.15 (2025) / 4,587.09 (2026)**; use actual SS if higher (NOT the legacy 4,104).
- Employee Social Security 11%, no ceiling.
- Regional: Azores = flat 30% reduction on all brackets; Madeira = tiered reduction (30% up to 6th bracket, 15%/9%/3% on 7th/8th/9th).
- Joint taxation (quociente conjugal): tax = 2 × scale(combined / 2). Dependent credits (annual): €600 base (+€126 under 3, etc.).
- Sources: info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt (CIRS arts. 25, 68, 69, 78.º-A); seg-social.pt; IAS (Santander/official).
